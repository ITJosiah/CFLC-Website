History of CFLC

It all started from a small group of people in JNLWM – a non sectarian full gospel church under the shepherding of Rev. Oscar A. Bustos who at that time was already working with children through Children’s Ministry – feeding, teaching and other child care deeds that CFLC was birthed.

He then thought of how the church building be utilized more to become more useful aside from every Sunday Worship Service. His wife, Tita Diding, then desired of how to reach more children since she was already working with children through Child Care Center together with Mam Tintin and bring it into a formal school.

It was also a right timing when Mam Terry just got retired from public teaching that she decided to join the team. With them undergone an intensive Early Child Education Teacher’s Training in Laguna with IFL as the curriculum. It was then that the ball started to roll for CFLC.

June of 2007 was the first opening of school with only 27 pupils in PRESCHOOL - Nursery, PreKinder and Kinder levels.

Christian Foundational Learning Center (CFLC) is existing and operating as a non-stock, non-profit Christian school for Nursery to Kinder and Grade I to VI pupils.

Our Mission

CFLC is committed to provide quality Christian education for a holistic development of learners founded on Biblical values and principles.

Our Vision

We are a learning institution in raising a generation of Godly leaders by providing quality education.
